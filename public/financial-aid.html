Making your education affordable - Explore your options

Types of Financial Aid

Scholarships

Merit-based awards based on academic achievement, leadership, and extracurricular activities. No repayment required.

Grants

Need-based aid from federal, state, and institutional sources. Free money that doesn't need to be repaid.

Federal Loans

Low-interest loans with flexible repayment options. Subsidized loans don't accrue interest while in school.

Work-Study

Part-time jobs on campus to help cover educational expenses. Earn money while gaining valuable experience.
