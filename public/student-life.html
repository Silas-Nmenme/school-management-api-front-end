Housing Options

  • Traditional Residence Halls: Classic dorm living with community bathrooms
  • Suite-Style Housing: Private bathrooms and shared living spaces
  • Apartment Living: Independent living for upperclassmen
  • Theme Housing: Specialized communities based on interests

All housing includes high-speed internet, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security.

Campus Events

  • Homecoming Weekend: Annual celebration with parades and games
  • Cultural Festivals: Celebrate diversity through food and performances
  • Guest Speakers: Renowned leaders and experts visit campus
  • Club Fairs: Discover new organizations and activities

Weekly events keep campus life exciting and engaging throughout the year.
